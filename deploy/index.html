01 // HISTORY

Out of necessity, and
a random act of kindness.

Trade & Traverse started the way a lot of good things in Star Citizen do — out of necessity, and a random act of kindness.

nocturnalraven was running cargo when a pirate decided to make them the target. Forced down onto a nearby moon to save ship and cargo, they thought they'd made it out. Then the pirate found them, torpedoed the ship on the ground, and left them stranded, hurt, and very much screwed.

A distress beacon went up, not expecting much — and XiXGoldeyXiX was the one who actually showed up. That rescue turned into a solid friendship, regular play sessions, and eventually the realization that they wanted their own space in the verse. Not a rigid hierarchy or a hardcore roleplay guild — a community where players could trade, haul, mine, fight and explore without the drama.

Today Trade & Traverse brings together traders, haulers, miners, explorers and pilots of every experience level. Veteran or first-flight, you'll find a home here — making your mark among the stars, against all odds.
